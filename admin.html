Welcome to your CMS Dashboard

Here you can visually modify your personal biography, skill ratings, showcase projects, YouTube video listings, and add infinite custom sections on your homepage. All files are stored locally in your browser session memory. To persist updates to your website, click the "Save & Export JSON" button at the top-right and upload/commit the exported files into your GitHub Pages repo.

Deployment Checklist:

  • Modify any section cards using the dashboard panels.
  • Click the "Save & Export JSON" button at the top-right.
  • Replace the files inside /data directory (e.g. portfolio.json, projects.json) with the downloaded ones.
  • Push the commits to your GitHub Pages repository. The changes will reflect immediately!
